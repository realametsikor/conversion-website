Frequently Asked Questions

How do I compress an image to under 500 KB?

Upload your image to our compressor, adjust the quality slider until the output shows a file size under 500 KB, then download. You can preview the result to ensure quality is acceptable.

Will the image look blurry after compression?

Not necessarily. Modern JPEG compression at 70-80% quality produces virtually identical results to the original. Only at very low quality settings (below 40%) will you notice significant degradation.
