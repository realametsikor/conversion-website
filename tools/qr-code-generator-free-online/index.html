Frequently Asked Questions

Do QR codes expire?

Static QR codes (the kind our tool generates) never expire. The data is encoded directly in the pattern and works as long as the destination remains active.

Can I customize the QR code colors?

Yes. You can change the foreground and background colors to match your brand. Ensure strong contrast for reliable scanning.

What can I encode in a QR code?

URLs, plain text, WiFi credentials, contact information (vCard), email addresses, phone numbers, and more.
