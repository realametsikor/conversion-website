Frequently Asked Questions

Can I really compress a PDF to email-friendly (under 10 MB)?

It depends on the original content. Image-heavy PDFs can be dramatically reduced. Text-only PDFs are already compact. Our tool shows the output size before download so you can verify.

Does PDF compression affect text quality?

No. Text in PDFs is stored as vector data and remains perfectly sharp after compression. Only embedded images are affected, and our tool optimizes them while maintaining readability.

Is it safe to compress PDFs online?

Our tool processes everything in your browser — your PDF files never leave your device. This makes it safe for sensitive documents.
