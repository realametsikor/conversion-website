Frequently Asked Questions

What size should a passport photo be?

Requirements vary by country. US passport photos are typically 600×600 pixels, under 240 KB, in JPEG format. Check your specific country's requirements.

Can I take my own passport photo?

Yes, you can take your own photo following the guidelines (white background, front-facing, proper lighting) and use our tools to resize and compress it to meet requirements.

What if the compressed image is rejected?

Try a slightly lower compression level. For official documents, prioritize clarity over small file size. Ensure the image meets all dimension and format requirements specified by the agency.
