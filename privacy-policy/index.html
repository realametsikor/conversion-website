Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 1, 2026

1. File Uploads and Processing

Most of our tools process files entirely within your browser using client-side JavaScript. Your files are never uploaded to our servers in these cases. For tools that require server-side processing, uploaded files are automatically and permanently deleted within 1 hour of processing.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII). We use anonymous analytics to understand general usage patterns and improve our tools. No personal data, file contents, or calculation inputs are stored or tracked.

3. Cookies

We use minimal cookies necessary for site functionality. Third-party advertisement partners may use cookies to serve relevant ads. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

4. Third-Party Services

We may display advertisements from third-party ad networks. These partners may use cookies or similar technologies to personalize ads. We do not share any user data with these partners.

5. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please reach out through our contact page.
