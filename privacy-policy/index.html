Privacy Policy

Last Updated: Today

At PlatformName, your privacy and data security are our top priorities. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your information when you use our free online tools.

1. File Uploads and Processing

Client-Side Processing: Whenever possible, our tools (such as Image Compressors and Calculators) process your data entirely within your web browser. This means your files never leave your device and are never uploaded to our servers.

Server-Side Processing: For complex tools that require backend processing (such as certain PDF converters), your files are transmitted securely via HTTPS. We never store user files permanently. All uploaded files and generated outputs are automatically and permanently deleted from our servers within one (1) hour of processing.

2. Data Collection

We do not require you to create an account, and we do not collect personally identifiable information (PII) such as names, emails, or phone numbers to use our core tools. We may collect anonymous, aggregated analytics (like page views) to improve our platform's performance.

3. Third-Party Advertisements

To keep our tools free, we display third-party advertisements. These ad networks may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites on the internet. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting your browser's ad settings.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please visit our Contact page.
