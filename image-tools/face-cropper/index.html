How to Use

  1. Click "Select Photo" to upload a JPG, PNG, or WebP image from your device.
  2. Choose a passport photo preset: 2x2 inch (US), 35x45 mm (EU/UK), or 51x51 mm (India/China).
  3. Drag the crop rectangle over your face. Use the corner handles to resize it while maintaining the correct aspect ratio.
  4. Optionally enable "White Background" to replace the area around your crop with a clean white fill.
  5. Select your preferred output format (PNG or JPG) and click "Crop & Download" to save the photo.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are my photos secure?

Yes. This tool processes images entirely in your browser using the HTML5 Canvas API. Your photos never leave your device and are never uploaded to any server.

What passport photo sizes are supported?

The tool includes three common presets: 2x2 inches (600x600px at 300 DPI, used for US passports), 35x45mm (413x531px, used for EU and UK documents), and 51x51mm (602x602px, used for India and China visas). The crop overlay locks to the correct aspect ratio for each preset.

Can I use this for visa and ID card photos?

Yes. The preset dimensions cover the most common international passport and visa photo requirements. You can use any of the three presets depending on the country and document type you need.

What does the white background option do?

When enabled, the exported photo will have a solid white background behind the cropped area. This is required by many passport and visa authorities for official photos.

What output formats are available?

You can export your cropped photo as PNG (lossless, larger file) or JPG (compressed, smaller file). Both formats are widely accepted for online document applications.
