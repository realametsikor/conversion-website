How to Use

  1. Click "Select Image" to upload a JPG, PNG, or WebP photo from your device.
  2. Click and drag on the image to select the area you want to crop for your profile picture.
  3. Choose a shape (circle or square) and your desired output size from the dropdowns.
  4. Optionally add a border by setting a width in pixels and picking a color.
  5. Click "Generate Profile Picture" to see a preview of your result.
  6. Click "Download PNG" to save your finished profile picture.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are my photos uploaded to a server?

No. All processing is done entirely in your browser using the HTML5 Canvas API. Your images never leave your device and are never sent to any server.

What sizes are available for profile pictures?

You can export at 100x100, 200x200, 400x400, or 500x500 pixels. These cover the most common profile picture requirements for social media platforms, forums, and messaging apps.

Can I make a circular profile picture?

Yes. Select "Circle" from the shape dropdown to crop your image into a perfect circle. The transparent corners are preserved in the downloaded PNG file.

What format is the output image?

Profile pictures are exported as PNG files. PNG supports transparency, which is important for circular crops where the corners need to be transparent.

How do I add a border to my profile picture?

Set the "Border Width" to a value greater than 0 (up to 20 pixels) and choose your preferred border color using the color picker. The border will appear around your cropped profile picture.
