How to Use

  1. Upload a background image by dragging and dropping it onto the workspace, or click "Select Image" to browse your device. You can also click "Start with Solid Color" to skip the image upload.
  2. Type your headline text into the text field. The canvas preview updates in real time.
  3. Adjust the font size using the slider (24px to 80px) and pick a text color with the color picker.
  4. Choose the text position from the dropdown: Top, Center, or Bottom of the thumbnail.
  5. Optionally change the background color if you started with a solid color or want a colored backdrop.
  6. Click "Download PNG" to save your finished thumbnail at 1280x720 resolution, ready for YouTube.

Frequently Asked Questions

What size are the thumbnails created by this tool?

All thumbnails are generated at 1280x720 pixels, which is the recommended resolution by YouTube for custom thumbnails with a 16:9 aspect ratio.

Do I need to sign up or pay to use this tool?

No. This YouTube Thumbnail Maker is completely free and requires no signup, account, or payment. Just open the page and start creating.

Are my images uploaded to a server?

No. All processing happens entirely in your browser using the Canvas API. Your images never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy.

Can I create a thumbnail without uploading a background image?

Yes. Click "Start with Solid Color" to begin with a blank canvas. You can pick any background color and add your headline text on top of it.

What font is used for the headline text?

The tool uses the bold Impact font, which is a popular choice for YouTube thumbnails because of its wide, attention-grabbing letterforms. A black stroke outline is applied for readability on any background.
