How to Use

  1. Click "Select Image" or drag and drop a JPG, PNG, or WebP photo into the upload area.
  2. The tool automatically center-crops your image to a square. Use the zoom slider to adjust the crop area.
  3. Drag the image to reposition what is visible inside the crop frame.
  4. Select your desired output size: 320x320 (Instagram standard), 110x110 (display size), or enter a custom dimension.
  5. Check the circular preview to see how your DP will look on Instagram.
  6. Click "Download Instagram DP" to save your perfectly sized profile picture as a PNG file.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the correct size for an Instagram profile picture?

Instagram profile pictures are stored at 320x320 pixels. However, they are displayed at 110x110 pixels on mobile and 150x150 pixels on desktop. Uploading at 320x320 ensures the highest quality across all devices.

Are my photos uploaded to a server?

No. All image processing happens entirely in your browser using the HTML5 Canvas API. Your photos never leave your device and are never sent to any server, ensuring complete privacy.

What image formats are supported?

You can upload images in JPEG, PNG, and WebP formats. The output is always saved as a high-quality PNG file, which preserves sharpness and is ideal for profile pictures.

Why does Instagram crop my profile picture into a circle?

Instagram displays all profile pictures in a circular frame. While the actual uploaded image is a square, only the area inside the circle is visible. This tool shows you a circular preview so you can position your face or subject perfectly within the visible area.

Can I use this tool on my phone?

Yes. This tool is fully responsive and works on mobile browsers. You can upload photos directly from your camera roll, adjust the crop, and download the resized DP right on your phone.
