How to Add Filters to Photos Online

  1. Click Select Image or drag and drop a JPG, PNG, or WebP photo into the upload area above.
  2. Choose a quick aesthetic by clicking one of the Filter Presets (e.g., Grayscale, Sepia, Vintage, Blur).
  3. Fine-tune the result using the Manual Adjustments sliders to dial in the perfect brightness, contrast, and color saturation.
  4. Preview the filtered image in real time directly on the canvas.
  5. Click Download Image to save the final filtered, full-resolution image to your device as a high-quality PNG.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does applying filters reduce image quality?

No! Our tool applies the filters using direct pixel manipulation at the exact resolution of your original image. The downloaded file preserves the full quality of your original photo, ensuring crisp, lossless results.

Are my photos uploaded to a server?

Absolutely not. We value your privacy. All image processing happens entirely inside your web browser using HTML5 Canvas and JavaScript. Your photos never leave your device and are never sent to our servers.

Can I combine multiple filters and adjustments?

Yes. You can select a base filter preset (like Vintage) and then further adjust the brightness, contrast, and saturation sliders to customize it. All adjustments stack together and preview in real time.
