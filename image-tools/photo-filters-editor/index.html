How to Use

  1. Click "Select Image" or drag and drop a JPG, PNG, or WebP photo into the upload area.
  2. Choose a filter preset by clicking one of the filter buttons (Grayscale, Sepia, Vintage, etc.).
  3. Fine-tune the result using the Brightness, Contrast, and Saturation sliders.
  4. Preview the filtered image in real time on the canvas.
  5. Click "Download PNG" to save the final filtered image to your device.

Frequently Asked Questions

What filter types are available?

The tool offers 10 filter presets: Original, Grayscale, Sepia, Vintage, Warm, Cool, High Contrast, Brightness Boost, Invert, and Blur. You can also manually adjust brightness, contrast, and saturation with sliders for precise control.

Does applying filters reduce image quality?

Filters are applied using direct pixel manipulation on an HTML5 Canvas at the original image resolution. The downloaded PNG preserves the full resolution of your original image with the filter applied, so there is no quality loss from compression.

Are my images uploaded to a server?

No. All processing happens entirely in your browser using the Canvas API and JavaScript. Your images never leave your device and are never sent to any server. Your privacy is fully protected.

What image formats are supported?

You can upload JPEG, PNG, and WebP images. The filtered result is always downloaded as a high-quality PNG file, which supports lossless compression and transparency.

Can I combine multiple filters and adjustments?

Yes. You can select a filter preset and then further adjust the brightness, contrast, and saturation sliders. All adjustments are applied together and previewed in real time, so you can combine effects to achieve the exact look you want.
