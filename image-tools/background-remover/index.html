How to Use

  1. Click "Select Image" or drag and drop a JPG, PNG, or WebP photo.
  2. Wait a few seconds while the AI model automatically detects the subject and removes the background.
  3. Preview the result. Toggle between "Result" and "Original" to compare.
  4. Choose a replacement background: transparent (default), white, black, or any custom color.
  5. Click "Download PNG" to save the final image.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does this tool upload my images to a server?

No. The AI model runs entirely in your browser using WebAssembly. Your images are never uploaded to any server — all processing happens locally on your device.

How does the AI background removal work?

The tool uses a neural network (machine learning model) that has been trained to distinguish foreground subjects from backgrounds. It automatically identifies people, animals, products, and objects, then removes the background in one step — no manual clicking or color selection required.

Why does the first use take longer?

On first use, the AI model files (~40 MB) are downloaded to your browser and cached. Subsequent uses are much faster because the model loads from your browser's cache.

What types of images work best?

The AI works well with portraits, headshots, product photos, animals, and objects with clear subjects. It handles complex backgrounds, hair details, and semi-transparent areas better than manual color-based tools.

What format is the downloaded image?

The result is saved as a PNG file which supports full transparency. If you choose a transparent background, the removed areas will be fully transparent in the downloaded file.

Is there a file size limit?

The tool works best with images up to 20 MB. Very large images may take longer to process depending on your device's capabilities. For the fastest results, images under 5 MP (e.g., 2500 x 2000 pixels) are recommended.

Can I use this for commercial purposes?

Yes. The tool is free for both personal and commercial use. Since all processing happens in your browser, there are no usage limits or watermarks.
