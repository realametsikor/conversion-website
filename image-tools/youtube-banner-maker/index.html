How to Use

  1. Upload a background image by clicking "Upload Background Image" or dragging a file onto the drop zone, or pick a preset gradient or solid color.
  2. Type your channel name in the "Channel Name" field and adjust the font size and color to your liking.
  3. Optionally add a tagline below your channel name with its own font size and color controls.
  4. Use the "Show safe area guide" checkbox to see which part of the banner is visible across all devices (TV, desktop, tablet, mobile).
  5. Click "Download Banner (PNG)" to save your finished 2560x1440 channel art. Upload it directly to YouTube.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the recommended YouTube banner size?

YouTube recommends uploading channel art at 2560x1440 pixels. This tool creates banners at exactly that resolution so your art looks sharp on TVs, desktops, tablets, and phones.

What is the safe area on a YouTube banner?

The safe area is a 1546x423 pixel region centered on the banner. Text and logos placed within this zone are guaranteed to be visible on all devices, including mobile phones. Content outside this area may be cropped on smaller screens.

Are my images uploaded to a server?

No. Everything runs entirely in your browser using the HTML5 Canvas API. Your images and text never leave your device and are never sent to any server.

What image formats can I use as a background?

You can upload JPG, PNG, and WebP images as backgrounds. The finished banner is always exported as a high-quality PNG file ready for YouTube upload.

Do I need to sign up or pay to use this tool?

No. This YouTube Banner Maker is completely free with no signup, no watermark, and no usage limits. Create and download as many banners as you like.
