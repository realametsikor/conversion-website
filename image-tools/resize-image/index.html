How to Use

  1. Click "Select Image" to upload a JPG, PNG, or WebP image from your device.
  2. View the original dimensions displayed automatically after upload.
  3. Enter your desired width or height. With "Lock Aspect Ratio" checked, the other dimension adjusts automatically.
  4. Click "Download Resized Image" to save the resized version as a JPEG file.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does this tool upload my images to a server?

No. All image processing is done entirely in your browser using the HTML5 Canvas API. Your images stay on your device at all times.

Can I resize to exact pixel dimensions?

Yes. Enter the exact width and height you need. Uncheck "Lock Aspect Ratio" if you want to set each dimension independently.

What format is the resized image?

Resized images are exported as high-quality JPEG files (quality 0.92) for optimal file size and compatibility.

Is there a maximum image size?

The tool can handle any image your browser can load. Very large images (over 50MP) may be slow depending on your device's memory.
