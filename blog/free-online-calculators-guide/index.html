22 Free Online Calculators for Finance, Academics & Everyday Use

Published on March 2, 2026 · 11 min read

Quick math should not require downloading an app or signing up for a service. ToolsNest now offers 22 free online calculators that handle everything from loan payments and retirement savings to GPA scores and unit conversions. Every calculator runs in your browser with no signup, no ads blocking your results, and no limits on usage.

This guide walks through every new calculator, organized by category, so you can find exactly the one you need.

Finance and Business Calculators

Whether you are planning a major purchase, running a small business, or managing personal finances, these calculators do the heavy lifting so you can make informed decisions.

Loan Calculator

The Loan Calculator computes monthly payments, total interest paid, and a full amortization schedule for any loan. Enter the principal amount, annual interest rate, and loan term to see exactly what you will pay each month and over the life of the loan. Whether it is a mortgage, car loan, or personal loan, knowing these numbers before you sign puts you in control.

Retirement Savings Calculator

How much will your retirement fund be worth in 20 or 30 years? The Retirement Savings Calculator projects your savings growth based on your current balance, monthly contributions, expected rate of return, and years until retirement. Seeing the power of compound interest visualized can change how you approach saving.

Savings Goal Calculator

Want to save for a down payment, vacation, or emergency fund? The Savings Goal Calculator tells you exactly how much to save each month (or week) to reach your target by a specific date. It accounts for interest earned on savings so you can see how even a small rate helps.

Break-Even Calculator

Every business needs to know when revenue will cover costs. The Break-Even Calculator determines the exact number of units you need to sell — or revenue you need to generate — before your business turns a profit. Enter fixed costs, variable cost per unit, and selling price per unit to get your answer.

Commission Calculator

Sales professionals and freelancers can use the Commission Calculator to quickly figure out earnings based on commission rates and sales totals. Supports flat-rate and tiered commission structures.

Markup Calculator

Pricing products correctly is critical for profitability. The Markup Calculator helps you calculate the right selling price based on your cost and desired profit margin, or reverse-engineer the margin from a known price.

Discount Calculator

Trying to figure out the final price after a 30% discount, or how much you save with a buy-one-get-one deal? The Discount Calculator handles single discounts, stacked discounts, and shows you both the savings amount and the final price.

VAT/Tax Calculator

The VAT/Tax Calculator adds or removes VAT or sales tax from any amount. Enter the pre-tax price and tax rate to see the total, or enter the total to extract the tax amount. Works for any country's tax rate.

Academic Calculators

Students deal with numbers constantly — grades, GPAs, and academic planning. These calculators make those tasks instant.

Grade Calculator

The Grade Calculator computes your overall course grade based on weighted categories like homework, exams, projects, and participation. Enter each category's weight and your score to see where you stand and what you need on the final to reach your target grade.

GPA to Percentage Converter

Different institutions use different scales. The GPA to Percentage Converter translates between the 4.0 GPA scale and percentage scores, supporting multiple grading standards. Essential when applying to international programs or comparing academic records.

Date, Time, and Life Calculators

These calculators answer the everyday questions that come up more often than you would expect.

Age Calculator

The Age Calculator computes your exact age in years, months, days, hours, and even minutes from any birth date. Useful for forms, age verification, and milestone tracking.

Date Difference Calculator

How many days until your wedding? How many weeks since you started a project? The Date Difference Calculator finds the exact gap between any two dates in days, weeks, months, and years.

Time Zone Converter

Remote teams span the globe, and scheduling meetings across time zones is a constant headache. The Time Zone Converter converts any time between any two time zones instantly. It accounts for daylight saving time so you never accidentally schedule a call an hour off.

Pregnancy Due Date Calculator

The Pregnancy Due Date Calculator estimates your due date based on your last menstrual period or conception date. It provides the estimated due date along with trimester milestones to help you plan ahead.

Percentage Calculator

The Percentage Calculator handles every common percentage problem: what is X% of Y, X is what percent of Y, and percentage increase or decrease between two numbers. A simple tool you will use more than you expect.

Random Number Generator

Need to pick a random winner, generate a dice roll, or choose a random number for any purpose? The Random Number Generator creates truly random numbers within any range you specify, with options for integers or decimals.

Unit Converters

Converting between measurement systems is one of the most common online searches. These converters give instant results for the units people look up most often.

Height Converter

The Height Converter converts between feet and inches, centimeters, and meters. Type in 5'10" and instantly see the metric equivalent, or enter centimeters and get the imperial result.

Weight Converter

The Weight Converter handles conversions between pounds, kilograms, ounces, grams, and stones. Enter a value in any unit and see all conversions at once.

Speed Converter

The Speed Converter converts between miles per hour, kilometers per hour, knots, and meters per second. Useful for travel planning, sports analysis, and science homework.

Fuel Cost Calculator

Planning a road trip? The Fuel Cost Calculator estimates total fuel costs based on the distance, your vehicle's fuel efficiency (MPG or L/100km), and the current price of gas or diesel. Know what you will spend before you leave the driveway.

Data Usage Calculator

Streaming, video calls, music, and social media all consume data differently. The Data Usage Calculator estimates how much data your daily activities use so you can choose the right mobile plan and avoid overage charges.

Why Use an Online Calculator Instead of a Spreadsheet?

Spreadsheets are powerful, but they require setup time, formula knowledge, and a desktop application. Online calculators give you answers in seconds with no configuration:

  • Instant access: No software to open or formulas to write. Enter your numbers and get a result.
  • Mobile-friendly: Use them on your phone during a meeting, at a store, or while traveling.
  • No errors: Pre-built calculators eliminate formula mistakes. The math is always correct.
  • No account needed: No signup, no login, no saved data. Use the tool and move on.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are the calculator results accurate?

Yes. All calculators use standard mathematical formulas and handle edge cases properly. For financial calculators, results match what you would get from a spreadsheet using the same inputs and standard amortization formulas.

Can I use these on my phone?

Every calculator is fully responsive and works on smartphones, tablets, and desktop browsers. The interfaces are designed for touch input as well as keyboard.

Is my financial data stored anywhere?

No. All calculations happen in your browser. Your numbers are never sent to a server, stored in a database, or shared with anyone. Close the tab and your data is gone.

Can I bookmark a specific calculator?

Yes. Each calculator has its own URL that you can bookmark for quick access. For example, bookmark the Loan Calculator if you use it regularly.

Do the unit converters handle all measurement systems?

The converters cover the most commonly used units for each measurement type. If you need a conversion not currently available, check the All Tools page for related tools that might help.
