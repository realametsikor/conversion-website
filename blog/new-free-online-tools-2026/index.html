Why All Tools Are Free and Private

Every tool on ToolsNest processes data directly in your browser. Your files, text, and calculations never leave your device. There are no uploads to external servers, no accounts to create, and no data stored after you close the tab.

This matters because many "free" online tools actually upload your files to their servers, where they may be stored, analyzed, or shared. ToolsNest takes a different approach: all processing happens client-side using JavaScript, so your data stays on your machine.
