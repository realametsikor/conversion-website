How to Use

  1. Enter the date and time you want to convert using the date-time picker.
  2. Select the source time zone (the time zone of the entered time).
  3. Select the target time zone you want to convert to. Use the swap button to quickly reverse the conversion.
  4. Click "Convert Time" to see the equivalent time in the target zone, along with the time difference and a multi-zone comparison.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does this converter handle daylight saving time?

This converter uses fixed UTC offsets and does not automatically adjust for daylight saving time (DST). During DST periods, you may need to manually select the appropriate offset (e.g., EDT at UTC-4 instead of EST at UTC-5).

What time zones are supported?

The converter includes all major world time zones: HST, AKST, PST, MST, CST, EST, AST, GMT/UTC, CET, EET, MSK, IST (India), JST (Japan), AEST (Australia), NZST (New Zealand), and many more, covering offsets from UTC-10 to UTC+12.

What does the "Day Change" indicator mean?

When converting between time zones with large offsets, the date may change. The "Day Change" indicator tells you if the converted time falls on the same day, the next day, or the previous day compared to the source date.

How accurate is this converter?

The converter is accurate for standard time zone conversions based on UTC offsets. For critical scheduling across time zones, always confirm with a source that accounts for DST transitions and any regional time zone rules.
