How to Use the Age Calculator

  1. Click on the Date of Birth field and select your birth year, month, and day using the calendar popup.
  2. If you want to know how old you were (or will be) on a specific date, enter it in the Calculate Age As Of field. If you just want your current age, leave this blank.
  3. Click the Calculate Exact Age button.
  4. Instantly view your chronological age in years, months, and days. Scroll down to see your total days lived and the countdown to your next birthday party!

Frequently Asked Questions

How is the exact age calculated?

The calculator computes the chronological difference between your date of birth and the target date. It counts the complete years, then the complete months, and finally the remaining days, taking into account varying month lengths (like February having 28 days).

Does the calculator account for leap years?

Yes. The JavaScript logic behind the calculator accurately tracks leap years. If you were born on February 29 (a leap day), your birthday is calculated correctly, and the countdown adjusts dynamically depending on whether the current year is a leap year or not.

Can I calculate my age as of a past or future date?

Absolutely. Use the "Calculate Age As Of" input field to select any historical or future date. This is incredibly useful for finding out exactly how old you were when a specific life event happened, or determining your exact age at an upcoming graduation or retirement date.

Is my date of birth stored or saved anywhere?

No. We prioritize your privacy. All math and date calculations happen entirely inside your web browser. No personal data, including your date of birth, is ever sent to a server or stored in a database.
