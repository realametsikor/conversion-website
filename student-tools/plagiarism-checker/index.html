How to Use

  1. Single Text Analysis: Paste your text and check for internal repetition, overused phrases, and get an originality score.
  2. Compare Two Texts: Switch to compare mode and paste both texts. The tool detects shared n-grams and highlights matching passages.
  3. Click "Check for Plagiarism" to analyze. Results include an originality score, breakdown of repeated phrases, and highlighted matches.
  4. Use the highlighted output to identify and revise repeated sections.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does this tool check against the internet?

No. This is a client-side tool that checks for internal repetition within your text or compares two texts you provide. It does not search the internet or external databases. For web-based plagiarism detection, consider dedicated services like Turnitin.

How does the originality score work?

In single-text mode, the tool breaks your text into n-grams (sequences of 4+ words) and checks for repeated sequences. The originality score reflects what percentage of your text is unique. In compare mode, it checks how many n-grams from Text B appear in Text A.

What text length works best?

The tool works best with at least 100 words. Longer texts provide more meaningful analysis. Very short texts may not have enough content to detect meaningful patterns.

Is my text stored anywhere?

No. All processing happens entirely in your browser. Your text is never sent to any server or stored anywhere.
