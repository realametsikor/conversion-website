Frequently Asked Questions

How is reading time calculated?

Reading time = total words / reading speed (WPM). The default speed is 238 words per minute, which is the average adult reading speed according to research. Speaking time uses 130 WPM, the average conversational speaking rate.

What is the readability grade?

The readability grade uses the Flesch-Kincaid formula, which estimates the US school grade level needed to understand the text. It considers average sentence length and syllable count per word. A grade of 8 means an 8th-grader should understand it.

What is vocabulary density?

Vocabulary density (also called lexical diversity) is the ratio of unique words to total words. Higher density means more varied vocabulary. A density above 70% suggests rich, diverse writing, while below 50% may indicate repetitive language.

Is my text stored anywhere?

No. All analysis happens in your browser. Your text is never sent to any server.
