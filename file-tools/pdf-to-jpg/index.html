Frequently Asked Questions

Is this PDF to JPG converter free?

Yes, our PDF to JPG converter is completely free with no limits on conversions. No signup or registration required.

What quality options are available?

We offer three quality levels: High (best quality, larger files), Medium (balanced quality and size), and Low (smaller files, faster processing). High quality uses 2x scaling for crisp images.

Are my files secure?

Absolutely. Your PDF is processed entirely in your browser using JavaScript. No files are uploaded to any server, ensuring complete privacy.

Can I convert multi-page PDFs?

Yes! Each page of your PDF is converted to a separate JPG image. For multi-page PDFs, you can download all images as a convenient ZIP file.

What is the maximum file size?

You can convert PDFs up to 50MB. Since processing happens in your browser, very large PDFs may take longer depending on your device.
