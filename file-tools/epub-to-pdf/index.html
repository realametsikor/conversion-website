Frequently Asked Questions

What is an EPUB file?

EPUB (Electronic Publication) is the most widely supported ebook format. It is used by most ebook stores and readers, including Apple Books, Google Play Books, and Kobo. Converting to PDF makes your ebooks readable on any device.

Why convert EPUB to PDF?

PDF is universally supported on every device and platform. Converting EPUB to PDF ensures you can read your ebooks on any computer, tablet, or phone without needing a specialized ebook reader application.

Will the formatting be preserved?

The converter extracts the content from the EPUB and generates a clean, readable PDF. While complex layouts may be simplified, all text content and basic structure are preserved.

Is this tool free to use?

Yes, our EPUB to PDF converter is completely free. No sign-up required, no watermarks, and no conversion limits.

Are my ebooks kept private?

Your files are processed securely and are never stored on our servers. All conversion happens in your browser, and your data is removed when you close or leave the page.
