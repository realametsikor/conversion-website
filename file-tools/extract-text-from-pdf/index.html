Frequently Asked Questions

Can this tool extract text from scanned PDFs?

Yes, our tool uses OCR (Optical Character Recognition) technology to extract text from scanned documents and image-based PDFs. It works with both digital and scanned PDF files.

What languages are supported?

Our OCR engine supports English and many other Latin-based languages. For best results, ensure the document text is clear and legible.

Is the extracted text editable?

Yes, once extracted, the text is fully editable. You can copy it directly from the text area or download it as a plain text (.txt) file for further editing in any text editor.

Are my files secure?

Absolutely. Your files are processed in your browser and are never uploaded to our servers. Your documents remain completely private.

How accurate is the text extraction?

For digital PDFs (text-based), the extraction is nearly 100% accurate. For scanned documents, accuracy depends on the scan quality. Clear, high-resolution scans yield the best results.
