Frequently Asked Questions

Can I choose where page numbers appear?

Yes, you can place page numbers in six different positions: top-left, top-center, top-right, bottom-left, bottom-center, or bottom-right. The most common choice is bottom-center.

What number formats are available?

We offer three formats: simple numeric (1, 2, 3), with "Page" prefix (Page 1, Page 2, Page 3), and Roman numerals (i, ii, iii). Choose the format that suits your document style.

Can I start numbering from a specific page number?

Yes, you can set any starting number. This is useful when your PDF is part of a larger document and you need numbering to continue from a specific page.

Is this tool free?

Yes, adding page numbers to your PDF is completely free. No signup or account is required, and there are no limits on usage.

Will adding page numbers affect my document content?

No, page numbers are added to the margins of your document. All existing text, images, and formatting remain completely unchanged.
