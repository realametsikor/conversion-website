How to Add Subtitles to Video

  1. Click "Select Video File" or drag and drop an MP4 or WebM video into the upload area.
  2. Upload an SRT subtitle file or switch to the manual tab and type your subtitles in SRT format.
  3. Customize the subtitle appearance: font size, color, background, and position.
  4. Preview the subtitles on the video player to verify timing and style.
  5. Click "Burn Subtitles into Video" to permanently embed the captions.
  6. Download the new WebM video with hardcoded subtitles.

Frequently Asked Questions

What subtitle formats are supported?

This tool supports the SRT (SubRip) format, which is the most widely used subtitle format. SRT files contain sequentially numbered entries with start/end timestamps and the corresponding text.

What is the difference between soft and hard (burned) subtitles?

Soft subtitles are separate files that can be toggled on or off during playback. Hard (burned) subtitles are permanently embedded into the video frames. This tool burns subtitles into the video so they display on any player without needing a separate subtitle file.

How long does burning subtitles take?

Processing time depends on the video length, resolution, and your device performance. The video is played through a canvas and re-recorded in real time, so a 2-minute video takes roughly 2 minutes to process.

Why is the output in WebM format?

The tool uses the browser's built-in MediaRecorder API for client-side processing. Most browsers encode to WebM (VP8/VP9) natively. This avoids requiring any server-side processing or external libraries.

Can I customize the subtitle appearance?

Yes. You can adjust the font size, font color, background color, background opacity, and vertical position (top or bottom). Changes are reflected in the live preview before burning.

Is my video data secure?

Absolutely. All processing happens entirely in your browser using the Canvas and MediaRecorder APIs. Your video is never uploaded to any server.

Is this tool free to use?

Yes, our Add Subtitles to Video tool is completely free with no registration, watermarks, or usage limits.
