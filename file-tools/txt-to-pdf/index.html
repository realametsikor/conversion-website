Frequently Asked Questions

What font is used in the PDF output?

The PDF is generated using the Helvetica font, which is a clean, professional sans-serif typeface that is universally supported across all PDF readers.

Can I customize the page layout?

Yes, you can choose between A4 and Letter page sizes, select from four font sizes (10pt, 12pt, 14pt, 16pt), and pick narrow, normal, or wide margins to suit your needs.

Does the tool handle long text files?

Yes, the converter automatically splits your text across multiple pages with proper pagination. Long lines are wrapped to fit within the selected margins.

Is this tool free and private?

Absolutely. The conversion happens entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Your text files are never uploaded to any server. The tool is completely free with no limits.

What character encoding is supported?

The tool reads UTF-8 encoded text files. Most modern text files use UTF-8 by default. Special characters and accented letters are supported to the extent that the PDF font allows.
