How to Split a PDF

  1. Upload your PDF file by clicking "Select PDF File" or dragging it into the tool area.

  3. Click "Split PDF" to process your file.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I extract specific pages from a PDF?

Yes! Use the "Extract specific page range" option to pull out a contiguous set of pages, or use the "Custom split" option to select individual pages and ranges like "1-3, 5, 7-10".

Will splitting affect the quality of my PDF?

No, splitting a PDF does not re-encode or compress the content. Each resulting file maintains the exact same quality as the original document.

Is there a page limit?

Our tool can handle PDFs with up to 500 pages. The file size limit is 50MB. For very large documents, consider compressing the PDF first.

Is this service free?

Yes, our PDF splitter is entirely free. No sign-up required, no watermarks added, and no limits on how many files you can split.

What happens to my uploaded files?

Your files are processed securely and are never stored on our servers. Once you download the result or leave the page, all data is removed.
