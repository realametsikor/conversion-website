How to Merge PDF Files

  1. Click "Select PDF Files" or drag and drop multiple PDFs into the upload area.
  2. Arrange the files in the desired order by dragging them in the list.
  3. Click "Merge PDFs" to combine all files into a single document.
  4. Download your merged PDF file once processing is complete.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many PDF files can I merge at once?

You can merge up to 20 PDF files at a time. Each file can be up to 50MB in size. For larger batches, merge in groups and then merge the results.

Can I reorder the files before merging?

Yes! After adding your files, you can drag and drop them in the list to change the order. The final merged PDF will follow the sequence you set.

Will the quality of my PDFs be affected?

No. The merge process combines files without re-encoding or compressing them. All images, text, and formatting remain at their original quality.

Is this tool free?

Yes, this PDF merger is completely free with no limits on usage, no watermarks, and no registration required.

Are my PDF files secure?

Your privacy is our priority. Files are processed securely and are never stored on our servers permanently.
