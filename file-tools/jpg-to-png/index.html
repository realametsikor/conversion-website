Frequently Asked Questions

Why convert JPG to PNG?

PNG supports transparency (alpha channel) and uses lossless compression, meaning no quality is lost. This makes PNG ideal for logos, graphics, screenshots, and images that need a transparent background.

Does this tool actually convert my image?

Yes! This tool uses the HTML5 Canvas API to genuinely convert your JPG image to PNG format entirely in your browser. No server upload required.

Will my image quality change?

The PNG output preserves the same pixel quality as the JPG input. Since PNG uses lossless compression, no additional quality loss occurs during conversion. The PNG file may be larger because lossless compression is less efficient for photographic images.

Is my image uploaded to a server?

No. Everything happens locally in your browser. Your images never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy and security.

What is the maximum image size?

There is no strict file size limit since processing happens in your browser. However, very large images (over 50 megapixels) may be slow to process depending on your device.
