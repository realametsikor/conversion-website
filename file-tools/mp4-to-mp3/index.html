Frequently Asked Questions

What audio quality should I choose?

For most purposes, 192 kbps provides excellent audio quality. Choose 320 kbps for the highest possible quality, or 128 kbps if you need smaller file sizes (good for spoken content like podcasts).

How long does the conversion take?

Conversion time depends on the video length and your device. Most files under 100MB are processed within a few seconds to a minute.

Can I convert other video formats?

This tool is optimized for MP4 files, which is the most common video format. For other formats like AVI, MKV, or MOV, you may need a general-purpose video converter.

Is the audio quality affected by the conversion?

The audio quality depends on the source video and the bitrate you select. If the original video has low-quality audio, choosing a higher bitrate will not improve it beyond the original quality.

Is this tool free to use?

Yes, our MP4 to MP3 converter is completely free with no registration, watermarks, or conversion limits.
