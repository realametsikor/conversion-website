How to Convert Screenshots to PDF

  1. Click "Select Screenshots" or drag and drop one or more images (JPG, PNG, or WebP) into the upload area.
  2. Preview your screenshots as thumbnails. Drag to reorder or remove any you do not need.
  3. Choose your preferred page size (A4, Letter, or Fit to Image), orientation, and margin settings.
  4. Click "Generate PDF" to create your document. Each screenshot becomes its own page.
  5. Click "Download PDF" to save the finished file to your device.

Frequently Asked Questions

What image formats are supported?

This tool accepts JPG/JPEG, PNG, and WebP image formats. These cover the vast majority of screenshot formats across all operating systems and devices.

Can I combine multiple screenshots into one PDF?

Yes! You can select multiple images at once or add more after your initial selection. Each screenshot will be placed on its own page in the final PDF document.

Can I reorder the screenshots before generating the PDF?

Yes. After uploading your screenshots, you can drag the thumbnails to rearrange them in the order you want. You can also remove individual images by clicking the remove button on each thumbnail.

What page sizes are available?

You can choose A4 (210 x 297 mm), US Letter (8.5 x 11 inches), or Fit to Image which sets each page to match the exact dimensions of the screenshot.

Are my screenshots uploaded to a server?

No. All processing happens entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Your images never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy and security.

Is there a limit on how many screenshots I can convert?

There is no strict limit. However, since everything is processed in your browser, very large batches (over 50 high-resolution images) may be slower depending on your device capabilities.
