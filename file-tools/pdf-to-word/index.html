Frequently Asked Questions

Is this PDF to Word converter free?

Yes, our PDF to Word converter is completely free to use with no limits on the number of conversions.

Are my files secure?

Absolutely. Your files are processed in your browser and are never uploaded to our servers. Your privacy is fully protected.

Will the formatting be preserved?

Our converter preserves most formatting including tables, images, fonts, and layout. Complex PDFs with unusual layouts may have minor differences.

What is the maximum file size?

You can convert PDF files up to 50MB in size. For larger files, try compressing the PDF first using our Compress PDF tool.

Can I convert scanned PDFs?

This tool works best with text-based PDFs. Scanned documents (image-based PDFs) may require OCR processing for best results.
