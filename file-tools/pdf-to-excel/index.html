Frequently Asked Questions

Is this PDF to Excel converter free?

Yes, our PDF to Excel converter is completely free to use with no limits on the number of conversions. No registration required.

Will the table formatting be preserved?

Our converter does its best to preserve table structure, column alignment, and data formatting from your PDF. Complex layouts with merged cells may require minor adjustments.

Are my files secure?

Absolutely. Your files are processed in your browser and are never uploaded to our servers. Your data remains completely private.

Can I convert scanned PDFs to Excel?

This tool works best with text-based PDFs that contain selectable text and tables. Scanned documents (image-based PDFs) may require OCR processing for best results.

What is the maximum file size?

You can convert PDF files up to 50MB in size. For larger files, try splitting the PDF first using our Split PDF tool.
