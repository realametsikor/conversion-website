Frequently Asked Questions

Is this PowerPoint to PDF converter free?

Yes, our PowerPoint to PDF converter is completely free to use with no limits on the number of conversions. No signup or account is required.

Will my slide formatting be preserved?

Our converter preserves slide layouts, fonts, images, charts, and formatting. The resulting PDF will look exactly like your original presentation.

Are my files secure?

Absolutely. Your files are processed in your browser and are never uploaded to our servers. Your privacy is fully protected.

What PowerPoint formats are supported?

We support both modern .pptx files (PowerPoint 2007 and later) and legacy .ppt files (PowerPoint 97-2003). Both formats are converted seamlessly to PDF.

What is the maximum file size?

You can convert PowerPoint files up to 50MB in size. For larger presentations, consider compressing images within PowerPoint before converting.
