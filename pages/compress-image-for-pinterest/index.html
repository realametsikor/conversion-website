Frequently Asked Questions

What is the ideal image size for Pinterest?

For Pinterest, images should be under 500KB. This ensures fast loading, quick uploads, and the best visual experience on the platform.

Will compressing reduce image quality on Pinterest?

Our compressor uses balanced settings that maintain sharp visual quality while reducing file size. The difference is imperceptible on Pinterest's display.

What formats does Pinterest support?

Pinterest generally supports JPG, PNG, and WebP. Our compressor outputs optimized JPEG which is universally supported and offers the best compression ratio.

Can I compress multiple images for Pinterest?

Yes, simply upload and compress images one at a time. Each processed image can be downloaded immediately before uploading the next.
