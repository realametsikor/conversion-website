Frequently Asked Questions

How do I compress an image to exactly 340KB?

Upload your image to our compressor. It applies optimized JPEG encoding to shrink file size. If the output exceeds 340KB, resize the image to smaller dimensions first, then compress again. Combining resizing and compression gives precise control over the final file size.

Will compressing to 340KB ruin image quality?

The compressor uses a balanced quality setting that keeps images looking sharp for most uses including web pages, social media, and email. At 340KB, most photos remain visually clear with no noticeable artifacts.

What image formats can I compress to 340KB?

You can upload JPG, PNG, and WebP images. The tool outputs compressed JPEG files which offer the best balance of quality and small file size.

Is it safe to compress images in my browser?

Yes. The entire compression process runs locally in your browser using the HTML5 Canvas API. Your images are never uploaded to any server, ensuring complete privacy and security.

Can I compress multiple images to 340KB?

Yes. After downloading a compressed image, simply upload the next one. Each image is processed independently in your browser.
