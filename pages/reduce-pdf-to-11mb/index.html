Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reduce a PDF to under 11MB?

Upload your PDF to our compressor and select the appropriate compression level. For reaching 11MB, "Medium" compression typically reduces files by 40-60%.

Will reducing to 11MB affect document quality?

The compressor offers three levels. Low compression preserves nearly identical quality with 10-20% reduction. Medium is recommended for most uses with 40-60% reduction. High achieves maximum compression with minor visual changes to embedded images.

What if my PDF is already under 11MB?

If your PDF is already under 11MB, you can still compress it further or simply use it as-is. The tool will show you the original and compressed sizes for comparison.

Is the PDF compression secure?

Yes. Your files are processed securely and never stored permanently on any server. Privacy and security are top priorities.

Can I compress a scanned PDF to 11MB?

Scanned PDFs contain images, so they typically see the largest compression gains. Our tool is particularly effective at reducing scanned document sizes to 11MB and below.
