Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best PDF size for web?

Web PDFs should be under 1-2MB for fast download times. High compression is recommended. Our compressor lets you choose the compression level that matches your web requirements perfectly.

Will the compressed PDF work for web?

Yes. The compressed PDF maintains full compatibility. All text, images, and formatting are preserved — only the file size is reduced.

How much can I reduce my PDF for web?

Typical reductions range from 10-80% depending on compression level and original content. Image-heavy PDFs see the largest reductions.

Is the compression secure for sensitive documents?

Yes. Your files are never permanently stored on any server. The compression process prioritizes your privacy and security.
