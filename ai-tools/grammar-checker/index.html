How to Use

  1. Paste or type your text into the input area above.
  2. Click "Check Grammar" to analyze your text.
  3. Review each issue — the tool categorizes errors as spelling, grammar, punctuation, or style.
  4. Click on any suggestion to see details and the recommended fix.
  5. Copy the auto-corrected text or manually apply changes.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of errors does this detect?

The tool checks for common spelling mistakes, subject-verb agreement, capitalization, punctuation errors (double spaces, missing periods), commonly confused words (their/there/they're, your/you're, its/it's), and style suggestions (passive voice, wordiness).

Does this use AI or an external service?

No. The grammar checker uses a comprehensive rule-based engine that runs entirely in your browser. Your text is never sent to any server.

How accurate is it?

The tool catches most common errors effectively. For academic or professional work, it serves as an excellent first pass, though specialized AI-powered tools may catch more nuanced issues.

Is there a word limit?

No word limit. The tool processes text of any length, though very long texts (10,000+ words) may take a moment to analyze.
