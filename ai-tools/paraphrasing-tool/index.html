Frequently Asked Questions

How does this paraphrasing tool work?

The tool uses a rule-based approach with an extensive synonym dictionary, sentence restructuring patterns, and mode-specific transformations. It replaces words with appropriate synonyms while maintaining grammatical correctness and the original meaning.

Is this tool free?

Yes, completely free with no word limits, no signup, and no API calls. Everything runs in your browser.

Will it pass plagiarism detection?

While paraphrasing changes wording, you should always cite your sources. This tool helps you express ideas in your own words, but proper attribution is still required in academic work.

Which mode should I choose?

Use Standard for general use, Formal for essays and business writing, Fluency to smooth out rough drafts, Simplify to make text more accessible, and Creative for blog posts or casual content.
