How to Use

  1. Enter your topic or context in the text area. Be as specific as possible for better results.
  2. Select your target social media platform from the dropdown menu.
  3. Choose the tone that matches your brand voice or the mood of your post.
  4. Toggle the Call-to-Action and Emoji options based on your preference.
  5. Click "Generate Captions" and copy your favorite caption with one click.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Caption Generator work?

The tool uses a sophisticated template engine with hundreds of hooks, body structures, and CTAs tailored to each platform and tone. It combines your topic with platform-specific patterns to produce engaging, ready-to-post captions with relevant hashtags.

Are the generated captions unique?

Yes. Each generation produces three distinct captions by combining different hooks, body templates, and CTA styles. The randomization ensures variety every time you click generate, even with the same topic.

Does this tool respect platform character limits?

Absolutely. Each caption is generated with the target platform's character limit in mind. The character count indicator shows you exactly how long each caption is relative to the platform's maximum, so you know it will fit perfectly.

Is my data private?

Yes. Everything runs entirely in your browser using client-side JavaScript. Your topics and generated captions are never sent to any server or stored anywhere.
