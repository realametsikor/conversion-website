Frequently Asked Questions

How is reading time calculated?

Reading time is estimated based on an average adult reading speed of 200 words per minute. Results are rounded up to the nearest minute.

Does this count characters with or without spaces?

Both! This tool shows total characters (with spaces) and characters without spaces separately so you can use whichever count you need.

Is my text stored or shared?

No. All processing happens entirely in your browser. Your text is never sent to any server.

What is the character limit for a Twitter/X post?

Standard tweets are limited to 280 characters. The writing targets panel above tracks this for you in real time.
