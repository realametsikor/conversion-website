How to Use

  1. Type or paste your text into the input area.
  2. Select a voice from the dropdown. Different browsers offer different voices and languages.
  3. Adjust speed, pitch, and volume using the sliders to your preference.
  4. Click "Speak" to start listening. Use Pause/Resume to control playback.
  5. The progress bar shows the current reading position in your text.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which voices are available?

Voices depend on your browser and operating system. Chrome typically offers the most voices including Google's high-quality voices. Safari uses macOS/iOS system voices. Firefox uses the OS default voices. The tool lists all available voices on your device.

Can I download the audio?

The Web Speech API does not support audio export directly. The speech is generated in real-time by your browser. For downloadable audio, you'd need a server-side TTS service.

Does it support multiple languages?

Yes! The available languages depend on your browser and OS. Most systems support English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, and many more. Use the language filter to find voices in your preferred language.

Is there a text length limit?

Some browsers may have limits on the length of text that can be spoken at once. For very long texts, the tool automatically chunks the text to ensure smooth playback.
