Frequently Asked Questions

Does this tool use AI or send my data anywhere?

No. This bio generator runs entirely in your browser using client-side JavaScript. Your personal information is never sent to any server, API, or third party. Everything stays private on your device.

How are the two bio variations different?

Each variation uses a different sentence structure and wording approach while including the same core details. This gives you options to pick the style that best represents you, or you can mix and match phrases from both versions.

Will the bio fit my platform's character limit?

Yes. The generator is aware of character limits for each platform (e.g., 160 characters for Twitter, 150 for Instagram). It shows a real-time character count and a color-coded indicator so you know immediately if your bio fits within the platform requirements.

Can I use the generated bio as-is?

Absolutely. The bios are written in natural, professional language and are ready to paste into your profile. That said, we recommend giving it a quick personal review to ensure it captures your unique voice and any nuances that matter to you.
