Frequently Asked Questions

What is title case?

Title case capitalizes the first letter of major words while keeping minor words (like "the", "and", "of") lowercase, unless they start the title. This follows standard English style guides.

Which style guide does this follow?

The title case conversion follows common rules shared by AP, APA, and Chicago styles — capitalizing words of 4+ letters and keeping short articles, prepositions, and conjunctions lowercase.

Is there a character limit?

There is no hard limit. The tool processes text entirely in your browser, so it works with any length of text.
