How to Write a Great Cover Letter

  1. Personalise it — address the hiring manager by name if possible and mention the company specifically.
  2. Lead with your value — open with your strongest skill or achievement, not "I am applying for..."
  3. Match the job description — use keywords from the job posting to pass applicant tracking systems (ATS).
  4. Keep it concise — one page maximum. Hiring managers spend less than 30 seconds on a cover letter.
  5. End with a clear call to action — request an interview or follow-up meeting.
