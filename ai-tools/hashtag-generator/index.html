Frequently Asked Questions

How are the hashtags generated?

The tool analyzes your content for keywords, matches them against a curated database of 500+ hashtags organized by niche and popularity tier, and combines relevant terms into compound hashtags. Everything runs client-side in your browser -- no data is sent anywhere.

What do the three popularity tiers mean?

High Volume hashtags have massive reach but high competition (e.g., #photography). Medium Volume hashtags target your niche with moderate competition (e.g., #streetphotographytips). Low Volume hashtags are specific long-tail tags with less competition but highly targeted audiences (e.g., #urbanstreetphotographynyc).

How many hashtags should I use per platform?

Instagram allows up to 30 (10-15 recommended for engagement). Twitter/X works best with 2-3 hashtags. LinkedIn is optimal at 3-5. TikTok performs well with 3-5 targeted tags. YouTube benefits from 5-15 hashtags in descriptions. Our tool provides platform-specific guidance.

Can I mix hashtags from different tiers?

Absolutely -- and that is actually the recommended strategy. Using a mix of high, medium, and low volume hashtags gives your content the best chance of being discovered by both broad and niche audiences. The three-tier approach maximizes your content's visibility across different audience segments.
