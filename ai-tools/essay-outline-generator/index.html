How to Use

  1. Enter your essay topic or title in the text field above.
  2. Select the type of essay you are writing from the dropdown menu (argumentative, expository, persuasive, etc.).
  3. Use the slider to choose how many main body paragraphs you need (3 to 5).
  4. Optionally, add your thesis statement and any key points you want included in the outline.
  5. Click "Generate Essay Outline" to create a detailed, structured outline with Roman numeral formatting.
  6. Expand or collapse sections as needed, then click "Copy Outline" to copy clean formatted text to your clipboard.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of essays does this outline generator support?

This tool supports eight essay types: Argumentative, Expository, Persuasive, Analytical, Compare & Contrast, Cause & Effect, Narrative, and Descriptive. Each type generates a unique outline structure tailored to its specific requirements, such as counterargument sections for argumentative essays or chronological flow for narrative essays.

Do I need to provide a thesis statement?

No, the thesis statement field is optional. If you leave it blank, the tool will generate a suggested thesis based on your topic and essay type. However, providing your own thesis statement will produce a more personalized and focused outline.

Can I copy the outline to use in a word processor?

Yes. Click the "Copy Outline" button to copy the entire outline as cleanly formatted plain text with proper indentation, Roman numerals, and lettering. You can paste it directly into Google Docs, Microsoft Word, or any text editor.

Is my data stored or sent to any server?

No. This tool runs entirely in your browser using client-side JavaScript. Your topic, thesis, and key points are never sent to any server or stored anywhere. Everything is processed locally on your device.
