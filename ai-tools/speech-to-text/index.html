How to Use

  1. Select your speaking language from the dropdown menu.
  2. Click the microphone button to start recording. Your browser will ask for microphone permission.
  3. Speak clearly into your microphone. Text appears in real-time as you speak.
  4. Enable "Continuous" mode to keep recording after pauses, or disable it for single utterances.
  5. Use Copy, Download, or edit the transcript directly in the text area.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which browsers support speech recognition?

Google Chrome and Microsoft Edge have the best support for the Web Speech Recognition API. Safari has partial support. Firefox currently does not support this feature. For the best experience, use Chrome.

Is my voice data sent to a server?

The speech recognition processing is handled by your browser's built-in engine. In Chrome, audio may be processed by Google's servers for recognition. The transcribed text stays in your browser and is not stored by ToolsNest.

How accurate is the transcription?

Accuracy depends on microphone quality, background noise, accent, and speaking clarity. For best results, use a good microphone in a quiet environment and speak at a moderate pace.

Can I edit the transcript?

Yes! The transcript area is fully editable. You can click into it and make changes, fix errors, or add punctuation at any time, even while recording.
